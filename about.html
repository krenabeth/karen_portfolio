About Karen

After working as a librarian for thirteen years, I'm taking the great adventure of a career change into web development. The little bit of javascript I've learned has been a lot of fun.

I live in Commerce Township with my 7 year old son. I've also lived in Fenton, White Lake, and West Bloomfield, and know some of the good bike trails and beaches in those areas. I like hiking as well and spotting wildlife. This year we've been seeing sandhill cranes in our yard.

My cat Roosevelt likes to sit on reading material whenever possible. He's the best. Except when he's being a rascal.

Some favorite things... bike trails at Kensington and Indian Springs Metroparks... libraries in the metro Detroit area - the Milford Public Library and the Waterford Township Public Library... animals at the zoo - anteaters
